About The Series

YurrTCG Grail Box Series

YurrTCG is an independent mystery card company producing high quality repacks containing ONE (1) raw or graded Pokémon card per Grail Box. The YurrTCG team sources from card shows, eBay, Whatnot, auction houses, and other trusted channels to curate top-tier pulls for the TCG community.

Grail Box Series

  • The Grail Box Series features top-of-the-line modern chasers, Raw or Graded.
  • Each box has a guaranteed Pokémon Raw or Graded card, seeded by rarity.
  • Grail Box provides high tier raw or slabs for true collectors looking to expand their collections.
